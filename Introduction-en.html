About uniXecure Technology Corporation

Considering the growing demands for rapid digital transformation among global enterprises, the advancement of new technologies has introduced a plethora of previously unknown information security concerns. This, alongside the increasing significance of international and government regulations regarding information security compliance, and the urgent need to address the shortage of skilled information security personnel in institutions and enterprises, prompted the establishment of uniXecure Technology Corporation on January 1, 2022.

uniXecure Technology Corporation, formerly affiliated with the senior information security team of SYSTEX Group, has a proven track record of providing professional information security consulting services to over 400 customers. These services have helped these customers safeguard their core competitiveness. With nearly 100 companies, uniXecure has extensive experience in implementing information security solutions across various industries, including the public sector, production, manufacturing, high technology, and finance etc.

In addition to serving as an agent for renowned international information security brands, uniXecure specializes in tailoring information security protection structures based on the unique business processes of its customers. By understanding their specific requirements, the company ensures that the implemented security measures are aligned with their needs.

From the perspective of information asset protection, uniXecure offers comprehensive one-stop information security services to government agencies and enterprises. This includes proactive measures such as pre-event prevention to minimize potential threats before they occur, mid-event detection to identify ongoing security breaches, and post-event response to effectively address and mitigate any security incidents.

By leveraging these services, government agencies and enterprises can enhance their information security posture and reduce the risks associated with potential threats. uniXecure aims to provide holistic and customized solutions to ensure the protection of valuable information assets for its customers.

In addition to building a security ecosystem and providing customized solutions for users in different industries, uniXecure will combine the strengths of its agent products and continue to form alliances with more international security companies to introduce cutting-edge security technologies to serve more customers. Based on this, uniXecure will combine its own brand, MOC, to help customers achieve the goal of total security.

Furthermore, uniXecure has ambitions to enter the international market and promote Taiwan's information security technology services globally. Through its expertise and partnerships, uniXecure aims to establish a significant presence in the global information security industry, showcasing the capabilities and innovations of Taiwanese information security solutions on an international scale.
