HEIS
Human Error Insight System

Comprehensive information security awareness training platform integrate phishing exercises and education training; gain insights into human-related information security risks within enterprises, and strengthen corporate information security alertness effectively!

Contact Us

HEIS developed a comprehensive social engineering simulation and cybersecurity education training to balance the IT department's resource allocation effectively.

HEIS (Human Error Insight System) is an important representative solution of Unixecure Technology Corporation in the global information security market; it has phishing email and fraudulent website templates designed based on hacker thinking; provides highly flexible social engineering exercise task settings, and complete information security awareness training courses, as well as information security promotions and tests that can assist enterprises in accelerating the improvement of the information security awareness of their employees, and allow enterprises to integrate the information security culture into their daily tasks, establishing the last line of information security defense for enterprises.

Quickly and clearly understand the human factor awareness risks of tested employees, reducing the risk of compromised personnel within the organisation due to inadequate cybersecurity awareness.

Functional Features

Visual dynamic interface

Real-time presentation of phishing email exercise status and test subject response data through visual interfaces and interactive charts, allowing IT personnel to quickly grasp the exercise status and task progress.

Customised group settings

Edit test subject group lists and fields flexibly, and add personal information fields according to the information of company employees, such as birthday, gender, age and horoscope.

Flexible exercise rules

Ability to flexibly configure sending times, sending frequencies, send methods and phishing email templates based on organisational structure and the purpose of the phishing exercise. The phishing email templates can simulate exercise scenarios in different situations and generate diverse exercise outcomes.

Customised email templates

Information security administrators can quickly design template contents, upload images, embed links, and flexibly adjust the fonts, graphics, size, colors and sender email address through the built-in email and website editor of this system to easily simulate hacker scenarios and conduct BEC (Business Email Compromise) scams.

Consultant level exercise reports

Report templates were designed through cooperation with senior information security consultants, experts and scholars in Taiwan, to produce highly professional Chinese and English consultancy reports according to the different exercise results, able to satisfy the internal and external auditing needs of different levels of government units and enterprises.

Introduction Benefits

Train enterprise employees to develop long-term complete awareness and concept for information security

In the information security protection network, people are the weakest link; hackers exploit people’s perceived behavioural weaknesses to launch attacks. Therefore, enterprises should think ahead to avoid imbalance in information security protection by relying on the importing of information security technologies and tools, but they forget to improve the information security awareness of enterprise employees. HEIS (Human Error Insight System) can build long-term learning ladders for enterprises to cultivate the information security awareness of their employees through planned phishing exercises and information security training courses, to assist enterprises in strengthening their overall defence capabilities on information security.

Reduce the burden for IT units on social exercise operations

In the past, IT personnel had to spend large amounts of time cost to manually plan and execute social engineering exercise tasks step by step without the help of automated systems, for example:

  1. Customized designs of exercise templates
  2. Monitor the operations during the execution of exercise tasks
  3. Collect and organize the response data of test subjects after exercises were completed
  4. Create exercise task reports.

Many non-automated exercise processes greatly impact the human resources for enterprise information security and IT unit operations. HEIS (Human Error Insight System) provides optimised experiences for social engineering exercise practices; it understands the needs and expectations of enterprises and information security administrators, and uses easy automated Q&A steps to guide the completion of exercise task settings, and can quickly produce consultant level Chinese/English reports with the press of a button after the tasks are completed.

Connected with international information security frameworks and specifications

HEIS is connected with international information security frameworks to help enterprises get in line with information security compliances.

  • Item Seven under Article 12 of Taiwan’s Enforcement Rules of the Personal Data Protection Act: Units must promote awareness, education and training.
  • Chapter 4 – Article 18 of Taiwan’s Regulations on the Notification and Response of Cyber Security Incident: Government agencies shall cooperate with competent authorities which shall plan and conduct one notification exercise and two Email social engineering exercises annually.
  • NIST-PR.AT (Awareness and Training)-1: All users are informed and trained
  • CIS CSC (Critical Security Control) #l7: Implement a Security Awareness and Training Program
  • ISA 99 /IEC 62443-2-1:2009 3.2.4.2: Element group: Security policy, organization and awareness
  • ISO/IEC 27001:2013 A.7.2.2 (Information Security Awareness, Education & Training): All employees and relevant contractors must receive appropriate awareness education and training to do their job well and securely
