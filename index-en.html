24/7 Monitoring of Cybersecurity Threats

The MOC is capable of integrating various pieces of information into one dashboard, including traffic, logs, antivirus software, system resources (processor, memory, disk capacity, etc.), and system configuration (GCB, security updates, etc.). It visualizes these data points to assist users in monitoring the status of attacks, enabling them to make prompt and efficient decisions.
