SIVAS
Systex Inventory Vulnerability Alert System

Functions provided by the system include software asset listing, safety update record, and device listing for public services and general enterprises, where lists of information assets are compared with a vulnerability database to minimize hacker attacks via loophole repair and version update to mitigate mainframe weak points. The system also automatically interfaces with the government entities Vulnerability Analysis and Notice System (VANS) platform of the Technical Service Center for implementing asset inventory and risk assessment required by the Information Security Act, thereby helping to enhance the integral intensity of Information Security of the entities or the enterprises compliance with Information Security Act requirements.

Substantial Benefits of SIVAS

Easy Inventory of Information Asset Lists

Converts software assets into the standard CPE format to compare asset weaknesses; provision of statistical charts after periodical weakness comparison, with warning notice issued based on risk ratings.

Weakness Detection and Scope Control

Review potential weaknesses of respective information assets for prompt response to threats and affected versions after release of weaknesses, getting control of affected scope, and ensuring controllability of weaknesses.

Loophole Repair and Weakness Tracking

To identify weaknesses and to assist the user expediting responding actions, including updating patches, stopping service operation, consulting for service, etc. while continuing to track weakness remedy status, to maintain effectiveness of data content, and to minimize weakness control costs.

Upload VANS for Quick Compliance

Automatic upload to VANS via API; manual upload after exporting an adjusted asset report can also be performed, so as to ensure full coverage of asset inventory and risk assessment required by the Information Security Act.
