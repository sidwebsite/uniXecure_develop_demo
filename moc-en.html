MOC

Integrated personnel, technology and procedures to protect your information asset comprehensively

Contact Us

Year-round operation and monitoring services allow you to develop the core businesses of the organization without worries.

The rapid development of digital innovation economy also caused the information security of agencies and enterprises to be exposed to high risks, and there are endless hacker attacks, cyber extortions and scams. The government further elevated information security to national security levels, and the Cyber Security Management Act was officially implemented in 2019. Not only are there government compliance requirements, many indicator enterprises such as TSMC, Apple, Boeing and General Motors also use the information security assessment as the standard for auditing their suppliers.

The “MOC (Monitoring and Operation Center)” team combined the information security service experiences of government agencies all over Taiwan, analyzed the Cyber Security Management Act and the latest information security defense information results, and proposed information security operation and monitoring services that can fully meet the information security requirements of different levels of organizations and enterprises. In response to the shortage of information security talents, operation service concepts and hosting modes were adopted, and IT equipment maintenance capabilities was used with the information security incident resolution capabilities of the “Monitoring and Operation Center” (which was certified by the government and ISO 27001) to transform information “maintenance” services into information “maintenance and operation” services, to provide clients with comprehensive information security services, ensure the three major requirements of the clients, data confidentiality, integrity and availability around the clock, achieve organizational governance visions through information security management capabilities, and lay the foundation for sustainable development.

Advantages and Functions

Detection, analysis and processing, all in one!

The MOC information security team not only provides detection and monitoring, but it also uses ISO-certified systematic information security incident handling procedures to provide you with 24/7 protection and incident, disaster and loss reduction handling. With the decades of accumulated front-line information service experiences and technical capabilities of SYSTEX, we can assist you in eliminating the harm caused by information security threats quickly and efficiently.

Reducing the crucial response time of Mean Time To Detect (MTTD) and Mean Time To Repair (MTTR) significantly

The average time from penetration to detection of information security threats (MTTD) by traditional SOC services is 197 days, and another 69 days is required from detecting to solving the incident (MTTR). Every delayed detection and handling will cause additional risk of service interruption, and may result in huge financial losses or loss in business reputation that took years to build. MOC assists you in reducing the detection and solution time significantly from several hours to even several minutes.

Single window incident handling

MOC’s information security monitoring, management and operation system integrated exception notifications from various monitoring systems at the endpoints, compares them with the databases of each monitoring system to identify the information security incident level and handling method, and then provides exception incident warnings and recommends solutions to the clients in real time. Every second is crucial when it comes to handling information security incidents; if clients have concerns, they can contact the specialist at the central console service desk at any time and connect using a single window to improve communication efficiency.
