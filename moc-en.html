Providing 24/7 Monitoring Services to ensure uninterrupted operation for your company

The MOC (Monitoring and Operation Center) team, with the richest experiences in analyzing the Cyber Security Management Act and reviewing the latest information security defense, was formed through the collaboration of SYSTEX Group's cybersecurity operations, response, and R&D teams. The team has proposed information security operation and monitoring services that fully meet the information security requirements of various organizations and enterprises.

To address the shortage of information security talent, the team has embraced the Managed Security Services Provider (MSSP) model. This approach integrates IT equipment maintenance and is complemented by the Operational Service Center, certified in ISO 27001, ISO 20000, and ISO 27701. The center demonstrates high proficiency in resolving cybersecurity incidents. The primary goal is to ensure clients' three fundamental requirements: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability.

The Services and Advantages of MOC​

Detection, Analysis and Processing, All in one

The MOC services team provides not only detection and monitoring services but also employs ISO-certified systematic procedures in responding to security incidents​.

Decreasing Time for Detection and Response​

Traditional SOC services have an average time of 197 days from infiltration to detecting a cybersecurity threat (MTTD), and an additional 69 days from detection to resolving the incident (MTTR). Every delay in detection increases the risk of service interruption, leading to significant financial and company reputation losses. MOC helps you substantially reduce the time for detection and remediation.​

Incident Management with a Single Point of Contact​

MOC integrates data from various endpoints. When unusual incidents occur, professionals at the monitoring center would compare data across systems to determine whether it's a false alert or a real incident and provide our clients with real-time suggestions and solutions. Additionally, we offer a single-Point of Contact service to enhance communication efficiency, reducing the damage caused by attacks.​
