2022 / 08 / 01

uniXecure Corporation passed connectivity test by the joint defense monitoring vendor of the National Center for Cyber Security Technology, including G-SOC 1.0, G-SOC 2.0, and EDR connection testing!

Revision for the “Cyber Security Management Act” was completed in 2021/08, stating that the monitoring range of information and communication security threat detection and management mechanisms should include the information and communication equipment records, information services or application records of the handling contents, directory service systems or the crucial information and communication systems of the agencies in the “endpoint detection and response mechanisms” and “information and communication security protection”; the National Center for Cyber Security Technology of the Executive Yuan has formulated a new version of STIX format (G-SOC 2.0) in response to the amendment of the law, and currently the connectivity test by joint defense monitoring vendors includes two major items:

  1. Information and communication security protection, directory service system and crucial information and communication systems: This is the G-SOC 2.0 connectivity test item derived from the two additional targets included for monitoring after the Cyber Security Management Act was amended.
  2. Endpoint detection and response mechanisms (EDR): After the Cyber Security Management Act was amended, it required information and communication security threat detection management to include “endpoint detection and response mechanisms,” required government agencies to introduce “endpoint detection and response mechanisms,” and notifications should be able to be provided through information and communication security threat detection and management mechanism service providers.

uniXecure Corporation has completed the G-SOC 2.0 connectivity test; public service agencies that delegate uniXecure Corporation to perform information security monitoring services can return monitoring intelligence back to G-SOC more comprehensively and immediately!
